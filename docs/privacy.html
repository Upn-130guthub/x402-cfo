Last updated: 20 March 2026

2. What we collect

The x402-cfo npm package runs entirely on your infrastructure. We do not collect, transmit, or store any data from the npm package. All payment ledger data stays on your machine.

This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services. We load fonts from Google Fonts, which may cause your browser to send your IP address to Google LLC. See Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Optional Dashboard Sync

No wallet keys, payment credentials, or raw transaction data are ever transmitted. Full details will be published before the feature launches.
